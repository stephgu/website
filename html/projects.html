Projects

Here's some stuff I did in my free time/school.

Sugar Rush - 2013

Created a game with a team of 10 about a boy who has to save his beloved Candyland from being invaded by the evil vegetables. Designed and created the game vision/mechanics, coded parts of the game (weapon combination, player movement, etc), drew the vegetable animations, and managed a team of 10 people over a quarter semester.
Play the game! / Github / Dev blog
Fibers - 2014

Created a game with a team of 4 about a kid wrapped up in a world of fibers. A puzzle platformer in which you must use fibers to create new terrain to traverse your environment. Designed the game vision and created the puzzle levels.
Play the game! / Github / Dev blog
Space Escape - 2014

Created a game with a team of 4 about traveler stranded in space. The traveler uses the momentum that is created from throwing objects out of the ship to navigate. But he only has a certain amount of resources he can use before running out. What will he do??
A mobile real-time strategy game. Designed all aspects of the game concepts, game mechanics, and game vision.
This game was meant for mobile so no playable...but, here's Our promo video! / Github
Lumi - 2013

Created a game prototype with a team of 3 about a lamp, Lumi, who is stuck in the dark. Lumi needs to rescue all her lightbulb friends by using her light to guide traverse the terrain. Designed and created the game concepts and mechanics. Coded dynamically generated platforms, light effects, bulb collection, light meter, and all animations.
Play the game! / Github
Body Runner - 2013

Created a infinite runner platformer prototype with a group of 3 about a stick figure who has lost all his limbs. As he runs he gains parts of his body back and along with it, special abilities of having fully functioning limbs again! Designe game concepts/game mechanic and coded the infinite runner as well as some of the body collections/abilities.
Play the game! / Github
